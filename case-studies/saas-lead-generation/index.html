Project Overview

Designed and implemented enterprise-level lead generation strategy for B2B SaaS client, focusing on account-based marketing, LinkedIn outreach automation, and thought leadership content. The project successfully reduced cost per lead by 60% while significantly improving lead quality and conversion rates.

Challenge

The SaaS client faced several critical challenges:

  • High cost per lead ($2,500+) with low conversion rates
  • Poor lead quality and high churn rates
  • Ineffective targeting of enterprise decision-makers
  • Lack of scalable lead generation process
  • Need for account-based marketing approach

Strategy & Implementation

1. Account-Based Marketing Framework

Developed comprehensive ABM strategy targeting 200+ enterprise accounts, creating personalized messaging and content for each decision-maker persona within target organizations.

2. LinkedIn Outreach Automation

Built sophisticated LinkedIn automation sequences using advanced targeting, personalized messaging, and multi-touch follow-up strategies that increased response rates by 300%.

3. Thought Leadership Content

Created industry-specific content including whitepapers, case studies, and executive insights that positioned the client as a thought leader and generated inbound leads.

4. Lead Qualification & Nurturing

Implemented advanced lead scoring and nurturing sequences that improved lead quality and increased conversion rates from 15% to 45%.
